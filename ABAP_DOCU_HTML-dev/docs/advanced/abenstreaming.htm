Streaming

Streaming describes how streams are passed. Streams are represented by instances of special classes. The transfer of streams takes place by calling the relevant methods of the object.

Properties of Streams

Streams are classified by the following properties:

  • Stream kind

    Data streams and filter streams
  • Stream direction

    Reader streams and writer streams
  • Stream type

    Binary streams and character streams
