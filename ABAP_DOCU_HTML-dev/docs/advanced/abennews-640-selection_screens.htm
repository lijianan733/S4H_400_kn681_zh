Selection Screens in Release 6.40


1. Arrangement of Radio Buttons



Modification 1

- Arrangement of radio buttons

As of Release 6.40, with the addition RADIOBUTTON to statement PARAMETERS, defined radio buttons are displayed in the first item of the selection screen and the relevant text to the right. Before Release 6.40, radio buttons were displayed to the right of the text.
